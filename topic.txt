1. Best topics for this engine
Good fits (factual, visual, B-roll-rich):

Technology: "How AI chips are made", "The rise of quantum computing"
Business/Finance: "Why inflation happens", "How Tesla disrupted car manufacturing"
Science: "How CRISPR gene editing works", "The James Webb telescope discoveries"
History: "The fall of the Berlin Wall", "How the internet was invented"
Nature/Environment: "Why coral reefs are dying", "How wildfires spread"


2. How to write a good topic description
The topic field goes directly into the ideation prompt. More detail = better script.

Weak:

"AI"

Strong:

"How large language models like GPT-4 are trained — covering data collection, compute costs, and what makes them hallucinate, for a tech-curious audience"

Template:

[What it's about] — covering [key angle 1], [key angle 2], and [key angle 3], for [target audience]

The target_audience field also helps — e.g. "tech-curious general public" vs "software engineers" vs "high school students" will change the tone and vocabulary the writer uses.
